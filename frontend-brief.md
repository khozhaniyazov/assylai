# Assyl AI Landing (Figma Build Brief)

This brief captures the current landing structure and content so you can rebuild in Figma Make from scratch without missing anything. It avoids visual specs and fixed colors; focus on layout, hierarchy, and meaning.

## Overall goal
- Convey trust, local credibility, and fast progress for children’s speech and development support.
- Push a single primary action: book a free diagnostic consultation (WhatsApp + form).
- Keep the flow clear: pain → proof → services → plans → process → specialist → CTA → FAQ.

## Top-level layout
- Single-page landing with stacked sections.
- Strong hero with split layout: text left, image right.
- Multiple proof blocks between hero and services.
- Consistent card-based layout for services, reviews, and plans.
- One primary CTA repeated in hero, services highlight, and final form.
- Floating WhatsApp button anchored at bottom-right on all screens.

## Brand tone and style direction (no color specs)
- Warm, medical‑credible, child‑friendly, premium but approachable.
- Use clean, readable typography with strong Cyrillic/Kazakh support.
- Soft gradients or textured background shapes in hero to avoid flatness.
- Rounded cards, gentle shadows, and calm spacing to build trust.

## Fonts and language guidance
- Use fonts that support full Cyrillic/Kazakh characters.
- Headings: confident and slightly condensed appearance.
- Body: highly readable, neutral sans.
- Avoid overly decorative or thin fonts.

## Navigation / Top info
- Not a full nav menu; a single top location line near hero badge.
- Location text: “Маңғыстау қ. • Маңғышлақ 84 үй”.
- Include a small location icon.

## Floating WhatsApp

- Links to WhatsApp with a prefilled message: “Сәлеметсіз бе! Диагностикаға жазылғым келеді.”
